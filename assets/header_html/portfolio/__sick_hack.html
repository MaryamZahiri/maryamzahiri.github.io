Sick Solution Award:

Describe:

Developed a generative AI basketball coach designed to enhance shooting technique and posture through the integration of computer vision, a LIDAR sensor, and an AI avatar. This project aimed to address how generative AI can contribute to making the world a healthier place within a tight 48-hour timeframe.
This project showcased the potential of AI in sports, enabling athletes of all levels to refine their skills while promoting physical health and wellness.

Innovative Technologies:
• Computer Vision: Utilized Python to analyze basketball shooting techniques through visual feedback.
• LIDAR Sensor: Implemented a Sick Sensor Intelligence LIDAR sensor to gather depth and spatial data for accurate analysis.
• AI Avatar: Created an interactive AI avatar to provide real-time coaching tips and feedback on shooting techniques.

Actions:

Python Development: Developed computer vision algorithms in Python and Colaborated to analyze shooting form and posture.
Camera Integration: Integrated stereo Sick Intelligence cameras to capture player movements during shooting.
Driver Testing: Conducted tests to ensure the LIDAR sensors and cameras worked seamlessly together to provide accurate feedback.

Responsibilities:

Focused on the development of computer vision algorithms in Python using Google pose estimation model, ensuring effective analysis of shooting techniques.
Managed the testing and integration of stereo cameras using python and driver installations for precise performance tracking. Addressed the challanges to use different stereo cameras

Goal:

To explore how AI and computer vision can enhance athletic performance and promote healthier lifestyles by providing personalized coaching.
To create a tool that not only benefits professional athletes but also supports recreational players in improving their game.
